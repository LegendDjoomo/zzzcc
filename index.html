Your Privacy Is Sacred

Zcriptures Local Data Policy

Zcriptures is 100% offline and privacy-first.

  • We collect zero personal data — no name, no email, no tracking.
  • All notes, highlights, bookmarks, and settings are saved only on your device using IndexedDB.
  • No cloud sync = maximum privacy (and your data stays yours forever).
  • If you uninstall or clear app data, everything is permanently deleted — your responsibility to back up if needed.

We don’t sell your faith. We protect it.
